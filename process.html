Our Process

A clear, professional approach that makes getting your new website simple and stress-free.

We've refined our process over years of working with professional service businesses. Every step is designed to be efficient, transparent, and respectful of your time.

You'll always know exactly where your project stands and what comes next. No surprises, no confusionâ€”just a straightforward path to a website you'll be proud of.

01

Discovery

We start by understanding your business, your clients, and your goals.

What Happens

  • Initial consultation to discuss your business and objectives
  • Review of your current online presence and competitors
  • Discussion of your target audience and ideal clients
  • Clarification of project scope and timeline

What You Provide

  • Information about your business and services
  • Examples of websites you admire
  • Brand materials (logo, colors) if available
  • Any existing content you want to include

Duration

1-2 business days

02

Design

We create the visual foundation of your website based on your brand and goals.

What Happens

  • Development of design concepts for your review
  • Creation of page layouts and visual hierarchy
  • Selection of typography, colors, and imagery style
  • Presentation of designs for your feedback

What You Provide

  • Feedback on design concepts
  • Approval of final design direction
  • Any specific preferences or requests

Duration

5-7 business days

03

Development

We build your website with clean, professional code that performs flawlessly.

What Happens

  • Translation of designs into functional web pages
  • Implementation of responsive design for all devices
  • Integration of contact forms and other functionality
  • Optimization for speed and search engines

What You Provide

  • Final content and images (if not included in package)
  • Review of progress at key milestones

Duration

7-10 business days

04

Review

You review the completed website and we make refinements based on your feedback.

What Happens

  • Presentation of the completed website for your review
  • Testing across devices and browsers
  • Implementation of your requested revisions
  • Final quality assurance checks

What You Provide

  • Thorough review of all pages and functionality
  • Consolidated feedback and revision requests
  • Final approval for launch

Duration

3-5 business days

05

Launch

Your new website goes live, and we ensure everything works perfectly.

What Happens

  • Deployment of your website to your hosting environment
  • Configuration of domain and SSL certificate
  • Final testing in the live environment
  • Handoff of all files and documentation

What You Receive

  • All website files and assets
  • Documentation for future updates
  • Post-launch support as included in your package

Duration

1-2 business days
