Common Questions

How long does a website take to complete?

Most projects are completed within 3-4 weeks from kickoff. Executive packages may take 4-6 weeks depending on complexity. We'll provide a specific timeline during our initial consultation.

Do I need to provide content?

You can provide your own content, or we can handle copywriting for you. The Professional and Executive packages include copywriting services. For the Essential package, copywriting is available as an add-on.

What about hosting?

We can recommend reliable hosting providers, or you can choose your own. We also offer managed hosting as an optional service for clients who prefer a hands-off approach.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. We typically split payments into two installments: 50% to begin the project, and 50% upon completion before launch.

What if I need changes after launch?

All packages include post-launch support for any issues. For ongoing updates and changes, we offer maintenance plans or can handle requests on an as-needed basis.
